## 4. Non-Negotiables

1. Kairove is not MMD-only.
2. First production line is ordinary AI format reproduction.
3. ManualSeedScout is auxiliary; autonomous scouting is core.
4. Research assets, local assets, and generated assets stay separate.
5. Every source/asset needs provenance.
6. Every major decision needs DecisionLog.
7. Review-worthy uncertainty creates ReviewItem.
8. Permissions are capability switches, not linear levels.
9. Score weights must be visible, configurable, versioned, and recalculable.
10. Learning suggestions require approval before becoming rules.
11. Do not fake external APIs.
12. Do not auto-publish unless permissions and platform setup allow it.
13. Detailed planning drafts are not implementation approval.
